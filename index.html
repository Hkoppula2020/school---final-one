Campus Life

School Farm!

This farm was established on July 1, 1963!

Hale students in the Agriculture program work together to maintain the grounds of our farm as well as care for the plants and animals that live here. Horticulture students learn about plant biology and care and complete hands-on, practical projects around the farm. Landscaping students focus on garden design, proper plant placement and care. Animal Husbandry students feed and groom animals, clean their enclosures and learn about their specific physical, dietary and housing requirements.



School Cafeteria

This is where students eat their lunch!

The school cafeteria was established on July 1, 1963. It serves all LAUSD lunches.



School Yard!

This is where many activities take place!

All P.E. classes take place on the school yard. There are many fundraisers on the yard. There is a basketball court, soccer field/football field, and volleyball field.
